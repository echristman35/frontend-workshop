the village

The Village started as a small cafe and art gallery but has quickly become a force for all things local- art,
music and food. Everything you experience at The Village is from your community: the produce from farms
just down the road, the art that fills their walls comes from all over the Brazos Valley, and the live music that
brings in amazing regional talent.
